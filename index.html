Hi! My name is Fabian Reyes. I'm from Antofagasta, Chile, and I'm 21 years old. I'm enthusiastic about learning new things—for example, learning to code! One of the things I love—and I'm currently working on—is practicing the piano. It's very difficult, but I enjoy challenges!

I served a mission in the Argentina Buenos Aires East Mission, and I returned about a year ago. Serving a mission completely changed my life. I would not be how I am today if I hadn't decided to serve the Lord. I have the opportunity to serve as a Temple worker in the Antofagasta Chile Temple, where I live, which is one of the greatest blessings I have received, as I am able to attend the Temple with my family every week.

I also have recently been called to serve as an institute teacher, and as someone who loves teaching the scriptures, this calling brings me great joy.

Chile

Chile is known for its large territory. It is bordered by Peru to the north, and Bolivia and Argentina to the east. On the western side, we can see the Easter Island (Rapa-Nui) where the famous moais are located. Chile also has part of Antarctica, which is why it is called a tricontinental country.
